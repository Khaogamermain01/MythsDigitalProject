The temple of Hecate|1|2|3|4|She visited the temple of Hecate, whom which she was a high priestess off, She prayed to Hecate that she could be free from whatever that ails her. Behind the scenes, Hecate hears her cries, and pleads with Hera to allow her high priestess whom which is so devote to be free from her schemes. Hera begrudgingly agreed, for she did not necessarily require Medea to be in-love with Jason to complete her goal. Medea was freed for the Affects of Eros.
